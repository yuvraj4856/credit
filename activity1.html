What is an IoT application?

A network of IoT devices that can allow them to communicate and exchange data with other devices over the internet.

What are the main features needed?

  • Real-time
  • Connectivity
  • Security
  • Dynamic nature

What are some examples?

  • Smart home
  • Smart city
  • Self-driving car
  • Farming
  • Traffic management
  • Retail purpose
