What does smart mean?

Smart can also be called as intelligent. Something that is equipped with positioning and communication technologies and is integrated into a communication network.

What are the main features needed?

  • Security and surveillance
  • Climate control
  • Fire detection and fire safety
  • Water monitoring

What are some smart building scenarios?

  • An IoT device that can find the person who tries to sneak into the house wrongfully.
  • Fire detection with their safety.
  • A solar system that can always face the sunlight.
