What are the high-level features?

An IoT device that can find the unknown if they try to enter the home at night. For this, we need face detection which can detect the unknown person and if they find, switch on the alarm and can also show the live scenario.

What are the parts needed?

  • Camera
  • 2 Raspberry Pi
  • LED screen
